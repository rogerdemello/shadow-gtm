# Shadow GTM — 90-Second Demo Script

A tight, judge-facing run. Goal: land the **one loop** —
`live web → AI reasoning → a play a rep can run today` — and end on the War Room.

**Before you record:** run one scan first so pages are fetched and signals exist
(cached), then refresh. Keep a backup recording in case a live scrape is slow.
Use `ANTHROPIC_MODEL=claude-sonnet-4-6` for snappier live runs.

---

| Time | On screen | What you say |
|---|---|---|
| **0:00–0:10** | Dashboard, empty-ish. | "Every revenue team burns hours manually researching competitors. The intelligence is on the web — but it's blocked, fragmented, and stale. **Shadow GTM** unlocks it." |
| **0:10–0:20** | Click the preset chips → HubSpot, Clay, Apollo, Gong appear on the watchlist. | "I drop in four competitors I want to watch." |
| **0:20–0:42** | Click **▶ Run intelligence scan**. Signals stream into the live feed; the status dot pulses. | "One click. **Bright Data's Web Unlocker** pulls their live pricing pages, the **SERP API** pulls news, reviews and buying-intent chatter, and **Claude** reasons over all of it. Signals stream in live — pricing moves, hiring spikes, sentiment, intent." |
| **0:42–1:00** | Hover a high-score signal — point at the **"why it matters"** line and the opportunity score. | "Here's the difference. Most tools say *'competitor changed pricing.'* Shadow GTM says **why it matters** — *they're moving upmarket, their SMB base is now exposed* — scores the opportunity, and hands the rep the exact play." |
| **1:00–1:12** | Scroll to **AI recommended plays** (ranked). | "Every signal, ranked by opportunity. This is a prioritized action list, not a dashboard to go read." |
| **1:12–1:28** | Click **⚔ War Room** → type *"Attack HubSpot in the SMB market"* → plan renders. | "And the closer — War Room. I give it a directive, and it writes a full attack plan grounded in the live signals: weaknesses, pricing gaps, who to hit first, a 30-day campaign, and an outbound opener." |
| **1:28–1:35** | Back to dashboard / logo. | "We turned the live web into a real-time GTM intelligence layer for AI agents. Built on Bright Data. That's Shadow GTM." |

---

### Backups / fallbacks
- **Scan feels slow on stage?** Pre-run it; the feed persists. Narrate over the
  already-populated feed and just re-trigger one company.
- **Bonus beat (if time):** click **Battlecard** on a competitor to show
  one-click sales enablement from the same signals.
- **JS-heavy angle:** mention the **⚡JS** badge — those sites (e.g. LinkedIn)
  render through Bright Data's **Scraping Browser**, a third BD product in play.
